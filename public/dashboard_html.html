A Legacy of Financial Leadership

Mr. Atul Mittal is a practicing Insolvency Professional and the Founder of Aarsh Resolution Professionals Pvt Ltd (AARSHRP), an IPE registered with IBBI. With a foundation as a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (1988) and the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (1991), coupled with a B.Com (Hons) from Delhi University, he brings academic rigor to practical problem solving.

Over three decades, Mr. Mittal has worked closely with Business Leaders and Professional Boards in leading corporates, including significant international tenures in Indonesia. He is a collaborative team player passionate about mentoring and nurturing talent.
